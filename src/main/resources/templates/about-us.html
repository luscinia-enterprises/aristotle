Our Story

The development of Aristotle began with a group discussion between frustrated high school students. All of us had our issues with the educational system, and we were discussing how we could improve it. Many exciting ideas were brought up from many differing perspectives. We realized that unlike most aspects of modern life, the educational system has fundamentally remained unchanged for the last 150 years. That discussion lingered with us all for weeks, and we would occasionally bring it up. After extensive deliberation, we realized that as students, we possess a unique perspective on the woes of the current education system, and we had come to some substantive ideas on how we could improve it. In August of 2018, Luscinia Enterprises Assn. was incorporated, determined to provide high-quality educational services to anybody that has the drive to learn. We decided to start by catering our services to the education curriculum of our home province of British Columbia. Our dream was to use the promise of technology to improve the educational experience of students, parents, and teachers alike. At first we made slow progress, the 2018-2019 school year was our senior year; extracurriculars, university applications, and much more put the development of Aristotle aside. But slowly and steadily we made progress because of our passion for the project. We are almost ready to make aristotle available for everyone.
